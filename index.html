Help & Controls

Mouse Controls

  • Left Click + Drag: Rotate camera
  • Right Click + Drag: Pan camera
  • Scroll: Zoom in/out
  • Click on Planet: Select and view info
  • Double Click: Focus on planet

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space: Play/Pause simulation
  • R: Reset camera view
  • O: Toggle orbits
  • L: Toggle labels
  • +/-: Speed up/slow down time
  • 1-9: Select planet (1=Sun, 9=Neptune)
  • F: Toggle fullscreen
  • H: Toggle this help

Tips

  • Use the planet selector at the bottom to quickly navigate
  • Adjust time speed to observe orbital patterns
  • Toggle logarithmic scale for better visualization
  • Enable moons to see Earth's Moon and other satellites
